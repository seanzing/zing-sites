Castle Rock Nutrition is located downtown in Castle Rock, Colorado. CRN provides healthy, quick, and energizing meal replacement shakes and teas. Our protein shakes are healthy, filled with minerals and nutrients, a full meal-replacement, and great for breakfast, lunch, or post workouts. Along with our shakes comes an energizing and metabolism boosting tea, and an aloe shot for digestive health. Castle Rock Nutrition also offers nutrition and health coaching that is available locally and online! Our mission is to help our Castle Rock community feel better, move better, and live better - one shake at a time.
