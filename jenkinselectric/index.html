ABOUT US

At Jenkins Electric, we take pride in being South East Idaho’s premier electrical service provider, committed to delivering quality workmanship and ensuring customer satisfaction. With years of experience in the industry, our skilled electricians are well-equipped to handle a wide range of electrical services, from installations to repairs. We understand that electrical issues can arise unexpectedly, which is why we prioritize safety and efficiency in every project we undertake. Our mission is to deliver reliable electrical solutions that not only meet but exceed our clients' expectations.

﻿

We believe that exceptional service starts with a strong customer relationship. At Jenkins Electric, we work closely with our clients to understand their unique needs, providing tailored solutions that fit their specific requirements. Our dedication to transparency, professionalism, and integrity has earned us a reputation as a trusted partner for both residential, commercial and Industrial electrical projects. Whether you're looking to upgrade your lighting, install new wiring, or require emergency repairs, you can count on Jenkins Electric to brighten your home or business with expertise and care.

Electrical Installations
