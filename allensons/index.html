Our Story

Built for the Biggest Projects.
Now Here for Yours.

For over two decades, Clayton Allen honed his craft on the most demanding plumbing and heating projects in Colorado — hospitals, water treatment plants, international airports, and downtown high-rises.

When the pandemic hit in 2020, Clayton made a decision: take everything he'd learned at commercial scale and build a family business that brings that same level of quality to residential homes. The result is Allen & Sons — where your home gets treated like a $50 million build.

  • Fully licensed and insured in the state of Colorado
  • Transparent flat-rate pricing — no surprises, ever
  • Works with homeowners, contractors, and DIY enthusiasts
  • Quick estimates and same-week scheduling
