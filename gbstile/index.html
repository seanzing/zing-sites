About Us

A Family Tradition of Excellence

GBS TILE INC. was founded in 1998 by Fernando Guerrero right here in Houston, Texas. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted family business serving homeowners and businesses across the greater Houston area.

We specialize in all tile installations: kitchens, bathrooms, living areas, foyers, bedrooms, and full remodeling projects. Over 25 years of hands-on experience means every job is done right the first time.

When you work with us, you work with the Guerrero family. That means honest pricing, premium materials, and a team that genuinely cares about your home.
