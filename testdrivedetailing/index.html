Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our detailing services in Boulder.

What areas of Boulder do you service?
We provide mobile detailing throughout Boulder, Colorado and the surrounding metro areas. Our mobile service means we come to your home, office, or preferred location for maximum convenience.
How long does a typical detailing session take?
Exterior wash and wax typically takes 2-3 hours. Interior detailing takes 1-2 hours depending on vehicle size. Full detail packages usually require 4-5 hours. We can provide more precise estimates based on your vehicle's specific condition.
What is ceramic coating and how long does it last?
Ceramic coating is a premium protective layer that bonds to your vehicle's clear coat, providing superior protection against UV rays, dirt, and contaminants. Quality ceramic coating can last 2-3 years or longer with proper maintenance and care.
Do you offer any package deals or discounts?
Yes, we offer discounted rates for our Full Detail Package and special pricing for multiple vehicles. Contact us to discuss custom packages tailored to your needs and budget.
What happens if it rains during my scheduled detailing?
We monitor weather conditions and reschedule if heavy rain is forecasted. Light rain won't affect our work as we use professional-grade water repellent products. We can discuss flexible rescheduling options based on Boulder's weather patterns.
