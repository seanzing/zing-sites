The five pillars

The Condition Zero Protocol five pillars

Every pillar is a non-negotiable component of the protocol. Deployed on every account. In every environment. Without exception.

01

Color-Coded Zone Integrity

Eliminating cross-contamination at the source

To eliminate cross-contamination, Right Lane utilizes a strict color-coded protocol for all tools and chemicals. Equipment dedicated to sensitive production or medical zones never enters common areas, and vice-versa. Each zone has its own dedicated color-assigned tools — red for aerospace, blue for medical, yellow for technical, green for government and institutional, white for general commercial. This is not a preference. It is a non-negotiable protocol enforced before, during, and after every shift.

Red — Aerospace Blue — Medical Yellow — Technical Green — Government White — Commercial

02

Advanced Particle Capture

Industrial-grade HEPA/ULPA filtration on every account

Right Lane utilizes industrial-grade HEPA and ULPA filtration systems capable of capturing 99.97% to 99.999% of airborne particulates depending on zone classification. This ensures a controlled environment that meets the rigorous demands of advanced manufacturing and healthcare standards. The same filtration technology required for cleanroom operations is deployed across Right Lane’s full client base — not reserved for high-tier accounts.

03

ISO-Mapped Protocols

Cleaning sequences aligned to ISO 14644 international standards

Right Lane’s cleaning sequences are built to align with ISO 14644, focusing on particulate counts and environmental stability rather than just surface appearance. Our SOPs mirror the framework used in aerospace and pharmaceutical manufacturing — the same standards applied to NASA-adjacent facilities. Every account receives a written SOP that maps directly to the appropriate ISO classification level for their environment.

04

Audit-Ready Documentation — Chain of Cleanliness

Every shift. Every site. Documented.

Every shift is supported by a documented Chain of Cleanliness. Right Lane provides the data and logs necessary for your facility to pass safety inspections, compliance audits, and client walkthroughs with zero friction.

Deliverables on every account

  • Signed completion logs per visit
  • Chemical usage records
  • Equipment deployment logs
  • Monthly service summary reports
  • On-request compliance documentation packages

05

Specialized Technician Training

Not cleaners — contamination control technicians

Right Lane team members are not cleaners. They are technicians trained in contamination control, chemical safety, and the unique requirements of high-stakes commercial and industrial environments. Every field technician holds OSHA 10 certification minimum. Supervisors hold OSHA 30. All staff complete site-specific protocol training before their first visit and receive written SOPs for every account they serve.
